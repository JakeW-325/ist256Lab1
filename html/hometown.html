Dix Hills, USA

Location

Dix Hills is located in the western part of Suffolk County on Long Island, New York, positioned between the North and South Shores.

About

Weather

Dix Hills in Long Island has a mix of hot summers and cold, snowy winters. Rainfall is fairly even throughout the year

Population

Dix Hills, located in Long Island, New York, has a population of approximately 26,000 residents, offering a small-town feel within a suburban setting

Things To Do

  • Enjoy outdoor activities at Dix Hills Park, featuring an ice rink, pool, and trails.
  • Play a round of golf at the scenic 9-hole Dix Hills Park Golf Course.
  • Explore art at the Art League of Long Island with exhibitions and classes.
